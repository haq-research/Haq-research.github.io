## About

I am **Abdul Haq**, an AI Solution Architect and Senior AI Engineer with experience designing 
enterprise-scale AI and Generative AI platforms in regulated industries such as 
pharmaceuticals and healthcare.

My work focuses on building **production-grade AI systems** that combine:

- Large Language Models (LLMs)
- Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
- Agentic AI and multi-agent workflows
- Scalable data and ML infrastructure

I am particularly interested in **context and memory management for enterprise AI systems**, 
including long-term and short-term memory architectures that allow LLM applications to 
maintain context across complex workflows and long-running conversations.
